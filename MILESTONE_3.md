# BA590 Milestone 3: Implementation and Evaluation Evidence

Introduced in Week 5 and developed through sessions 5-1, 5-2, 6-1, 6-2.

## Purpose

Complete the bounded implementation, evaluate it with decision-relevant evidence, and document ethical data use, interpretation, and the professional report draft.

## Cumulative sequence

Carry forward the decisions and evidence from Milestone 2. Do not replace prior conclusions silently; record what changed and why.

## Required deliverables

- notebooks/final_analysis.ipynb or equivalent executable implementation and evaluation
- evaluation_report.md with baseline comparison, metric rationale, error slices, robustness, and uncertainty
- responsible_data_use.md with affected stakeholders, risks, controls, monitoring, recourse, and residual limits
- report_draft.md connecting claims to repository evidence and documenting reproducibility

## Evidence standard

Commit executable or inspectable artifacts, captured outputs, tests, and concise decision records. Another reviewer must be able to reproduce the result and determine the condition under which the conclusion should change.

## Submission

Push the completed repository and submit its URL in the matching Canvas assignment. Canvas is the official submission, rubric, feedback, and grade record.
