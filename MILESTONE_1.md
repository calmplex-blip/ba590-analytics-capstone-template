# BA590 Milestone 1: Business Problem and Project Charter

Introduced in Week 1 and developed through sessions 1-1, 1-2, 2-1, 2-2.

## Purpose

Define a consequential business decision, establish stakeholder and analytical context, and submit a feasible project charter with explicit acceptance criteria.

## Cumulative sequence

This is the first repository in the course sequence. Establish a clean, reproducible foundation for later milestones.

## Required deliverables

- problem_statement.md defining the business decision, affected stakeholders, intended use, constraints, and success criteria
- evidence_review.md comparing credible prior work and identifying the justified analytical gap
- project_charter.md with deliverables, acceptance criteria, risks, schedule, and review plan
- notebooks/feasibility_baseline.ipynb or equivalent executable feasibility evidence

## Evidence standard

Commit executable or inspectable artifacts, captured outputs, tests, and concise decision records. Another reviewer must be able to reproduce the result and determine the condition under which the conclusion should change.

## Submission

Push the completed repository and submit its URL in the matching Canvas assignment. Canvas is the official submission, rubric, feedback, and grade record.
