# BA590 Milestone 2: Data, Method, and Reproducibility Plan

Introduced in Week 3 and developed through sessions 3-1, 3-2, 4-1, 4-2.

## Purpose

Establish authorized data, a reproducible preparation pipeline, a justified baseline and analytical method, and proposal-review evidence.

## Cumulative sequence

Carry forward the decisions and evidence from Milestone 1. Do not replace prior conclusions silently; record what changed and why.

## Required deliverables

- data_contract.md documenting provenance, authorization, unit of analysis, quality, representation, privacy, and limitations
- method_decision.md comparing a baseline and candidate methods under shared business and technical constraints
- notebooks/data_pipeline.ipynb and notebooks/analysis_baseline.ipynb with tests and captured outputs
- presentation/proposal_review.md recording questions, decisions, and committed revisions

## Evidence standard

Commit executable or inspectable artifacts, captured outputs, tests, and concise decision records. Another reviewer must be able to reproduce the result and determine the condition under which the conclusion should change.

## Submission

Push the completed repository and submit its URL in the matching Canvas assignment. Canvas is the official submission, rubric, feedback, and grade record.
