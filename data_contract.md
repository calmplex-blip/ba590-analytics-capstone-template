# Data Contract

## Scope and accountable decision

- Which provenance, authorization, schema, semantics, unit of analysis, time boundary, quality, privacy, and change rules apply?
- Which populations or conditions are not represented?

## Reproducible evidence

Link every material claim to an authorized source, data state, contract,
pipeline step, test result, service signal, or stakeholder observation.

## Failure behavior, limits, and revision trigger

State what happens under duplicate, late, changed, invalid, unauthorized, or
unavailable conditions. Record what the current evidence does not establish
and the observation that would require this artifact to change.
